Who Are We?

Welcome To Youth Politica

Welcome to our organization, where we are committed to providing students in the Greater Toronto Area with the tools they need to become informed and engaged citizens. Our focus is on improving political literacy, and we believe that by providing opportunities for students to participate in public-speaking events, engage in conversations with statespeople and youth leaders, and express their opinions in writing, we can help to create a more informed and politically engaged community. Our goal is to provide a safe space for students to explore different perspectives and engage in respectful dialogue about important issues. We believe that by fostering a community of informed citizens, we can help to build a better future for all.








Our Mission

We are an youth-run organization dedicated to improving the political literacy of students in the Greater Toronto Area in an unbiased way.

Our Vision

We aim to diversify students’ political experiences through public-speaking events, conversations with statespeople and youth leaders, as well as through the expression of opinion in writing.
