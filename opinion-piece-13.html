The John Tory Affair: Was His Resignation Justified?

John Tory deserved what came after his affair was revealed Fozia Kahloon · Youth Politica · Posted: May 22 2023 John Tory (CBC)      Known as the “family man,” no one would have foreseen this scandal that caused John Tory to step down as mayor of Toronto. Tory had just started his third term as Toronto’s mayor when his secret affair was revealed. He admitted to an affair with a former employee at his office and apologized for any harm due to his family, staff, colleagues and residents of Toronto.

     However, this “apology” could've in no way been sincere. Tory had been married to his wife for 40 years, he has children and grandchildren. He knew what he was doing and who it would hurt, yet he chose to abuse his power and start an affair with a younger employee at his office. Not to mention, before leaving office Tory had ensured that his lover had found a job at a different office. This in itself shows that Tory does not care how his actions affected those closest to him, he only cared once it was out in the public.

     However, one must admit that despite his utter lack of good judgment when starting the affair, John Tory still managed to make the correct choice to step down as mayor. He fully deserved that as he had tarnished his reputation. No one would have continued to trust him after his secret affair was revealed. He went against his “family man” persona and shattered not only so many hearts, but the trust residents of Toronto, his office staff and family had in him. Works Referenced Draaisma, M.
