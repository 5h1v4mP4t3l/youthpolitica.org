As Anticipation for Trump's arrest grows, the U.S. Finds Itself in a Dilemma

Indictment of Donald Trump reveals political, social, legal secrets
Justin Tangpongprush · Youth Politica · Posted: May 22 2023
Donald Trump (Financial Times)      Former United States president Donald Trump was indicted with an official criminal charge. He is charged with paying hush money regarding an extramarital affair with Stephanie Clifford, who claims to have had an affair with trump during his 2016 presidential election. This is 100% going to send shockwaves in the political landscape of America, as this is an event that has not happened before, or at least to this scale. Donald Trump is going to run for president in 2024, and with a criminal charge looming over his head, it’s something to be noted in the coming years.

     Firstly, the impact of this happening is prominent, especially considering who this is happening to. Donald Trump is a former president of the United States, with loyal voters and tons of political and financial power. He is an incredibly controversial president, and the riots that happened on January 6th 2021 are a key example of that. The cause of those riots was the political fallout from his losing the election and calling on his supporters to fight against that decision. His supporters are insanely dedicated to him, and with this recent controversy, there are paths that they must take. Supporters of trump with either: Ignore this controversy and continue supporting him, Discontinue their support, or Protest against the charges. a protest might not be too out of the picture, as it happened before, and since he is planning on running again for office in 2024, this might change the number of people voting for him, especially since the affair happened during a presidential campaign, which shows weakness as a candidate. Such an event in the middle of a campaign is never a good look.

     Though this does not affect Trump as it might seem from a glance. The criminal charge that was given to him is going to hurt his election campaign right? There are many politicians who’ve had criminal charges against them and were unaffected by their charges. As well as that, Donald trump usually does not leave the republican voters' sights, as his other controversies and potential criminal charges did not sway them from supporting him. Donald Trump has already recovered in popularity from the controversy regarding a police search at his resort. It is likely that he will recover as he has before. A counterpoint to that is the competition in his own political party. He has shown a weakness that can be exploited by other republican candidates.

     In my opinion, I doubt this event will have any drastic changes to America’s political climate. Though all the building blocks for a new Jan 6 riot are on the table, I have doubts in my mind that Trump will do anything extreme like that again. Especially since the election is coming up. Because Trump is likely to not lose much support from this event and because the spotlight is on him, the best political decision is to go through the trial and disregard it with his new election. Though this might have been a large controversy, it's not something that will affect his candidacy much. Whether that’s a good or bad thing is up to your political views.
